Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information across our personal projects: Soil Trails, Money Trails, and Cloud Trails. Your data privacy and trust are important to us.

2. Information We Collect

  • Soil Trails: Publicly submitted soil samples, locations, and notes.

  • Cloud Trails: Account details and uploaded personal files.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to operate and improve our services. No data is sold or used for commercial gain. Specific use-cases include:

  • Authenticating users and securing access

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is encrypted in transit and stored securely. We follow modern best practices including:

  • Hashed passwords (never stored in plain text)
  • SSL/TLS encryption for all connections
  • Regular monitoring and restricted access to data

5. Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may disclose data only when:

  • Required by law
  • Necessary for performance (e.g., hosting services)
  • Explicit consent has been given by you

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or request a copy of your stored data
  • Request correction or deletion
